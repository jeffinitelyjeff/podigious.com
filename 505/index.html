It’s fightin’ time again! Kurata blows up DATS, the kids get new cellphones to beat up Kurata’s rival digi squad, and there’s a cool lion.

In this episode, Ashley continues to cry over Merukimon, Jeff looks at the bancho in BanchoLeomon, we discuss how bio-hybrid evolution compares to similar digivolution mechanics, and we discover what hybrid Friends characters each of bio-hybrids is.

Thanks for listening!
