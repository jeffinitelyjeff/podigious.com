Welcome to the Podigious Data Squad Fantasy Draft!

Try to guess our MVPs for the upcoming podcast episode by drafting a (DATS-style) team of 1 human and 1 Digimon. There are 3 ways to get points:

  • MVP Points: 1 point awarded for each host MVP in your draft picks.
  • WTF Points: 1 point awarded by each host to the draft spirit evolution that they find the most ridiculous.
  • Desired Points: 1 point awarded by each host to the draft spirit evolution they most wish was canon.
